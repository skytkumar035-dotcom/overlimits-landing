Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Overlimits collects when you use the Service, and how that information is handled. Overlimits is a solo-run business based in India, and this policy is written to be plain and specific rather than long for its own sake.

1. What we collect

  • Uploaded files — the spreadsheet or CSV file you upload for cleaning.
  • Email address — only if you opt in to the "processing delay" notification flow, so we can let you know when a longer job is finished. If you don't opt in, no email is collected.

2. How uploaded files are handled

Uploaded files are processed to perform the requested cleaning operations and are not permanently stored. Files exist only for as long as needed to process and return your cleaned output, after which they are removed.

3. No selling of data

Overlimits does not sell, rent, or trade uploaded files, email addresses, or any other personal data to third parties.

4. Right to deletion

Since uploaded files are not permanently stored, there is typically nothing to delete after processing completes. If you opted into email notifications and would like your email address removed, you can request deletion at any time.

5. Contact for privacy requests

For any privacy-related request, including deletion requests, contact support@overlimits.in.

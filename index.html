What it actually fixes

Not "AI magic." Specific, checkable fixes.

Every change Overlimits makes shows up in the audit report, so nothing happens to your data silently.

Dedup

Duplicate row removal

Finds exact and near-duplicate rows across your key columns (name, email, phone) and removes them, keeping the most complete record.

Contact

Email & phone validation

Flags and repairs malformed email addresses and normalizes phone numbers into a consistent, dialable format with country code.

Finance

Salary standardization

Converts mixed formats — "45k," "45,000/yr," "₹45000" — into one consistent numeric format you can sort, filter, and sum.

Location

Geo-detection

Fills in missing city, state, or country fields where they can be inferred from other columns in the row.

Report

Full audit report

Every cleaning run produces a companion report showing exactly which rows and fields were changed, and how.

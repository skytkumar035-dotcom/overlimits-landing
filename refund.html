Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy covers refunds and cancellations for Overlimits Pro subscriptions ($19/month). The Free plan has no charge and therefore nothing to refund.

1. 7-day money-back guarantee

If you're not satisfied with Overlimits Pro, you can request a full refund within 7 days of the date of your first charge. This guarantee applies to the first charge only.

2. How to request a refund

Send a refund request to support@overlimits.in within the 7-day window, and it will be processed back to your original payment method.

3. Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Once cancelled, you will not be charged on your next billing cycle, but you keep Pro access for the remainder of the cycle you've already paid for.

4. No partial refunds after the guarantee window

Outside the 7-day money-back window, Overlimits does not offer partial refunds for unused time mid-cycle. Cancelling stops future charges but does not refund the current billing period.
